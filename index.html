Our Story

Rooted in the Zambian Soil,
Growing Sustainably

Kapeso Investments Limited is a proudly Zambian farm business based in Ipafu, Chingola. We believe in nurturing the land the way our grandparents did — with patience, respect, and love. Every animal we raise, every vegetable and grain we grow, tells the story of dedication and hard work.

  • Certified healthy farm practices — no harmful additives
  • Premium pork from well-managed pig farms
  • Quality broiler chickens raised on balanced feed
  • Heritage grains and legumes grown traditionally
  • Direct farm-to-family delivery available in Chingola & Ndola
  • Transparent sourcing — you know exactly where your food comes from
