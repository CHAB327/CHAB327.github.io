KAPESO INVESTMENTS LIMITED — GALLERY FOLDER GUIDE
===================================================

Upload your own images into the matching sub-folder.
The website will automatically display them.

FOLDER STRUCTURE:
-----------------
gallery/
  vegetables/     → Images of fresh vegetables you sell
  pigs/           → Images of your pigs / pork products
  chickens/       → Images of your broiler chickens
  grains/         → Images of grains & legumes
  team/           → Photos of your farm workers / team
  farm/           → General farm landscape / facility photos

IMAGE TIPS:
-----------
• Recommended size: 800×600 px or larger (landscape works best for cards)
• Supported formats: .jpg, .jpeg, .png, .webp
• Keep filenames simple, no spaces (e.g. pig_1.jpg, chicken_farm.jpg)
• The gallery section on the website references these paths directly.
  After uploading, update the src="" values in index.html if needed.

EXAMPLE:
  <img src="gallery/pigs/pig_1.jpg" alt="Our Pigs" />
